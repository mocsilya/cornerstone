Our standard 'Pouch' options are supplied in a sealable kraft windowed pouch - ensuring all sweets stay fresh for longer. Depending on your sweet choice, the weight you receive when selecting our gift options will vary - we can however, guarantee they will be full to the brim! Our gift cubes hold between 400g and 600g and measure 10cm. Our jar options are either 11cm in height (holding between 300g and 600g) or our larger jar is 19cm in height (holding between 800g and 1.6kg).

Don't forget to choose one of our colourful, professionally printed labels included with both small and large gift jars.
